eLearning Platform is an online revision resource platform where easy access to educational content is provided. We aim to ensure that both learners and educators are well equipped with all the material that they would need. The resources can be accessed through both the Easy Elimu website and app. For reference purposes, they can also be downloaded in PDF format to ensure that they can be referred to at any time, anywhere. This wide range of material comprises of Past Papers, Revision Notes, Prediction Papers, Educational Videos and Q&A forums from Playgroup all the way to Form 4.
